DISCOVER BONSAI

A calm, beginner-friendly workshop idea for Avenir residents

Hello Avenir Community Team,

A few Avenir residents recently visited our bonsai garden in West Palm Beach and encouraged us to reach out about a possible beginner-friendly bonsai workshop for the community.

We are a family-run South Florida bonsai business and members of the Bonsai Society of the Palm Beaches. Our goal would be to offer residents a relaxing, educational, and hands-on experience, not a hard-sell event.

About Bonsai Florida

A local bonsai experience built around learning, calm, and community.

Bonsai Florida shares tropical bonsai trees, beginner projects, workshops, and educational content designed for South Florida homes and patios. We can adapt the workshop for a clubhouse, outdoor patio, lifestyle event, or family-friendly resident program.

Workshop Formats

Simple workshop options

Discover Bonsai

Introduction to bonsai history, care, styling, and live demonstrations.
 

Create Your First Bonsai

Hands-on beginner experience where guests can style and take home their own starter bonsai tree.
 

Florida Patio Bonsai

Tropical bonsai care and low-maintenance patio-friendly trees for South Florida homes.

Additional formats, including family bonsai day or Japanese garden inspiration displays, can be discussed if useful for the community calendar.
